Refund and Returns Policy

Our goal is to ensure every customer is satisfied with their purchase. If you are not completely happy with your order, we offer a straightforward refund and returns process.

Eligibility for Refunds

You may request a refund within 30 days of your original purchase date. To be eligible, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase such as an order confirmation email or receipt. Digital products and downloadable content are eligible for refund only if they have not been accessed or downloaded.

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, contact our support team with your order number and a brief description of why you would like a refund. Our team will review your request and respond within two business days. If your refund is approved, you will receive instructions on how to return the item if applicable.

Processing Times

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within five to seven business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Please note that your bank or credit card provider may take additional time to post the refund to your account.

Partial Refunds

In some cases, a partial refund may be offered. This applies to items that show signs of use, items returned without original packaging, or items returned after the 30-day window but within 60 days of purchase. Partial refund amounts are determined on a case-by-case basis.

Exchanges

If you received a defective or damaged item, we will arrange a replacement at no additional cost. Contact our support team with photos of the damaged item and your order number. Exchanges are processed within three business days of approval.

Non-Refundable Items

Certain items are not eligible for refund, including gift cards, clearance items marked as final sale, and personalized or custom-made products. Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error on our part.

Escalation

If you believe your refund request was not handled fairly, you may request an escalation. Escalated cases are reviewed by a senior support specialist within 48 hours. We are committed to resolving all disputes in a fair and timely manner.

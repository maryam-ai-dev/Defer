Shipping Delays: Causes, Timelines, and Escalation

We understand that waiting for a delivery can be frustrating, especially when there are unexpected delays. This guide explains the most common causes of shipping delays and what steps you can take.

Common Causes of Shipping Delays

Shipping delays can occur for several reasons. Weather events such as storms, floods, or extreme temperatures may disrupt transportation routes. High-volume periods like holidays or major sales events can cause carrier backlogs. Occasionally, customs processing for international shipments may take longer than expected. Address issues, including incomplete or incorrect shipping addresses, can also cause delays or failed delivery attempts.

Expected Delivery Timelines

Standard shipping typically arrives within five to seven business days from the date of shipment. Express shipping is estimated at two to three business days. International orders may take seven to fourteen business days depending on the destination country and customs processing. These timelines begin from the date the order is shipped, not the date it is placed.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's website to monitor your package's progress. If tracking shows no movement for more than three business days, please contact our support team.

What To Do If Your Order Is Delayed

If your order has not arrived within the expected delivery window, first check the tracking information for any updates. If the tracking shows a delivery attempt was made, check with neighbors or your building's front desk. If the package appears to be stuck in transit, contact our support team with your order number and tracking number.

Reshipment and Refund Policy for Lost Packages

If a package is confirmed lost by the carrier, we will offer either a full reshipment of the order or a complete refund. Lost package claims typically require five business days after the expected delivery date before they can be filed. Our support team will coordinate with the carrier to investigate and resolve the issue.

Escalation for Shipping Issues

If your shipping issue has not been resolved within five business days of contacting support, you may request an escalation. Escalated shipping cases are prioritized and reviewed by our logistics team. We aim to provide a resolution within 24 hours of escalation. For time-sensitive orders, please mention the urgency when contacting support so we can expedite the process.
